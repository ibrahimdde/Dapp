Ethereum currency hits all-time high

Ethereum, a crypto-currency, skyrocketed into 1,000 USD mark after the new year, which it was unable to pass for the last 3 years. We saw spikes around 1300 USD in the last three weeks, specialists say it will go up even further. There was a video card shortage in the last quarter of 2020 after the new graphics card series release, RTX 3xxx, and it will be even rougher as cryto-currencies such as Bitcoin-Ethereum have doubled, even tripled their prices.


Second-hand graphics card market is pretty much dead expensive, and we can see some older cards being more expensive then their superseders MSRP's, which is unlike anything seen. It is even worse than 2017 christmas, which was considered as the worst hit in the graphics card market in the last two decades until now.
