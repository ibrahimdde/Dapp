AMD RX 6000 series released finally, sold out in seconds as expected

AMD's newest RX 6000 series graphics cards finally are on sale, and was sold out in seconds thanks to the hoarders. You can find one from sites like Ebay for the double the MSRP price if you want, but it is probably not worth it for it's price.
It was teased back in the october of 2020 in one of AMD's conferences.
Prices of the cards are:
  • Their flagship card, RX 6800's MSRP is 579$
  • Their high end card, RX 6800 XT's MSRP is 649$
  • Finally, their enthusiast card, RX 6900 XT's MSRP is 1000$.
